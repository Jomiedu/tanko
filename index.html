Summary: Tanko collects minimal data needed to show you fuel prices. We don't sell your data. We use your location only to find nearby stations.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tanko, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account
  • Location Data: Your device location to show nearby fuel stations (only when you grant permission)
  • Price Reports: Fuel prices you submit to help other users
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display fuel stations near your location
  • Show fuel prices reported by the community
  • Award points and badges for your contributions
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Supabase: Our database provider for storing app data
  • Firebase: For push notifications and analytics
  • OpenStreetMap: For map display

4. Location Data

We request location permission to:

  • Find fuel stations near you
  • Sort prices by distance
  • Allow location-based price reports

You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database with row-level security
  • Password hashing for account security

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of location tracking
  • Request data export

7. Children's Privacy

Tanko is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app.

Need help with Tanko? Here are some common questions:

Tap on any fuel station on the map or in the list, then tap "Update Price" to submit the current price you see at the station.
